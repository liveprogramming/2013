contest

LIVE 2013 will award 3 prizes for the best live programming demos.

  • The first prize will be $500 and complimentary registration.
  • The second prize will be $150 and complimentary registration.
  • The third prize will be $50 and complimentary registration.

To submit, participants should send an url of a running demo or a video, and a blog post describing the demo by April 4th. For videos, please running time to under seven minutes.

Submissions and questions should be emailed to
chris.parnin@gatech.edu

To be eligible for prizes, winning participants must register and present the demo for the LIVE workshop. Participants are also encouraged to submit their demo as a tool demo paper by February 7th.

Notification for prizes will be announced on April 11th.
